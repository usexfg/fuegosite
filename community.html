About Our Community

Welcome to the official Fuego Network community space! This is where $XFG holders and enthusiasts come together to:

  • Discuss the latest developments in the Fuego ecosystem
  • Share ideas and suggestions for future improvements
  • Get help with technical questions
  • Stay updated on project news and announcements
  • Connect with fellow community members

To participate, you'll need a GitHub account. This helps us maintain a respectful, spam-free environment while keeping the platform simple and privacy-focused.

Community Guidelines

Please follow these guidelines to ensure a positive experience for everyone:

Be Respectful

Treat all community members with courtesy and respect, regardless of their opinions or experience level.

Stay On Topic

Keep discussions relevant to Fuego Network, cryptocurrency, and related technologies.

No Spam

Avoid promotional content, referral links, or repetitive posts that don't contribute to discussions.

Getting Started

To join the conversation:

  1. Create a GitHub account if you don't already have one
  2. Navigate to the comments section below
  3. Sign in with your GitHub credentials when prompted
  4. Start participating in existing discussions or create a new topic

All discussions are public and archived in our GitHub Discussions repository.
