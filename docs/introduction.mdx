Fuego is a privacy-first blockchain built on CryptoNote with ring signatures. It gives you financial tools that traditional systems can't offer: locked deposits that earn yield without revealing your balance, trustless cross-chain swaps, and on-chain liquidity with private LP positions.

## Core properties

**Privacy by default.** Ring signatures hide the sender in every transaction. Commitment deposits hide your balance and interest behind cryptographic commitments. No one can determine your deposit amount from on-chain data.

**No counterparty risk.** Atomic swaps are enforced by cryptography, not escrow services. Commitment deposits are enforced by the consensus rules, not a bank. Your funds are controlled by your keys.

**Transferable and compoundable.** Unlike traditional CDs, you can transfer a locked deposit to another party before maturity. You can also roll over a mature deposit to compound interest — the new deposit amount equals your principal plus accrued interest.

## Fee structure

Two separate fee pools fund different parts of the system:

| Fee | Source | Goes to |
|-----|--------|---------|
| 1% | XFG side of every atomic swap | CD interest pool |
| 0.3% | Every trade through the LP pool | Liquidity providers |

These are completely independent. CD interest comes from swap activity, not LP trades.
