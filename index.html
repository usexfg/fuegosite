    Our Vision

    We believe every form of time spent on fandom of any kind has value.
    Whether it be listening to your new favorite song, creating fan-art, watching
    your favorite films - video games, legos, superheroes, even pop culture in general;
    but specifically social aspects of fandom - for example, when you can confirm whether
    your friend's time spent watching- say... 8 seasons of an HBO series, is worth it.

    Too, so much of fandom has largely been centered around content produced by
    corporate behemoths, yet, in today's world where technology is ever-increasingly allowing
    anyone to be a content creator- we believe P2P fandom is just beginning.

    By utilizing the Fuego decentralized blockchain network & privacy cryptocurrency,
    along with layer-2s designed & built by Fuego's Elder Council- we, as fans & creators-
    will keep the true power of fandom (and money) where it belongs for good...

    at a peer to peer level.
