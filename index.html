    Our Vision

    We believe every form of time spent on fandom of any kind has value.
    Whether it be listening to your new favorite song, creating fan-made art, watching
    your favorite films- video games, superheroes, or just popular culture in general; and 
     especially the social elements of fandom - when, for instance, you can confirm whether
     your friend's time spent watching- say... 8 seasons of an HBO series, would be worth it.

    Moreover, so much of fandom as we know it has been centered around content produced by
    corporate behemoths, yet- in today's world where technology is ever-increasingly allowing 
    anyone to easily become a content creator- we believe P2P fandom is just beginning. 

    By utilizing Fuego's decentralized blockchain network & privacy cryptocurrency,
    along with layers built by the Elder Node Council- we, as fans & creators-
    are able to put the true power of fandom where it belongs for good...

     at a peer to peer level.
